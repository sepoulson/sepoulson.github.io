Why LIS?

I have always found great comfort in libraries. From when I was a young child trying to check out as many books as I was tall, to finding refuge from the crippling isolation of college, to now, libraries have always been a source of support for me. While I may be trying to get out of the teaching profession, I want to still help people learn and better themselves however I can and I feel like libraries are the best place to accomplish that goal.
